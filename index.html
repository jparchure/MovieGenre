Hello There.

I created this visualization as a part of my Human Computer Interaction class at the University of Iowa. It takes the movie viewing preferences dataset and draws a parallel coordinate plot based on the data. Here's the link to the hosted web page. Feel free to explore on your own. Some gentle nudges-

  • Click the y-axis of the bar chart
  • Click the axes of the PCP, click them individually or in a pair.
  • Click the lines of the the Parallel Coordinate Plot, again individually or in pair.

Have fun!

Thanks to the Department of Computer Science, University of Iowa for the webspace
